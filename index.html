JUST THE BEST

Workshops from experts

Ethical Hacking (Cyber Security)

Hands on Demonstration of Latest Hacking Techniques & tools. Personal Interaction with Sunny Vaghela. Ethical Hacking Toolkit(Includes PowerPoint Presentations,Tools,Ebooks). Unique Barcode to Access to HackTrack Portal for 2 years

IC Engine

Technologies like ABS, EBD, PGMFi, DTS-Fi, MPFI, VVTI. The workshop will introduce the students to the basics of engines and their working mechanism. The students will get to learn both the principles and their implementation in reality by assembling the intricate IC engines themselves.

Mind Controlled Robotics

Student will learn EEG technology. Get to know about alpha, beta, delta wave. Make your own bot which will work according to your brainwave. Receive large volumes of usable content within a compressed amount of time.

Android App Development

Android App development training program Learn the basics of Android and Java programming, and take the first step on your journey to becoming an Android developer! This course is designed for students who are new to programming, and want to learn how to build Android Apps.
